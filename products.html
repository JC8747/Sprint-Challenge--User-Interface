Inline

We've got inline boxes! When you need something that just drops right into your page and no time to fuss with width and height, inlines are perfect! To ensure they don't get too far out of place left and right padding are allowed.

Inline-Block

If you need that pesky element box to actually BE a box, inline-block can help you out. Our Inline-Blocks know to repsect width, height, and padding and will always sit side-by-side.

Flex Box

When you need to create a whole lot of content with exact placement but don't want to go insane figuring out margin and padding, Flex Box is avaiable! Flex Box is your best friend, with many built in options to get everything on your page looking nice and crisp. It's even inherently responsive when properly implemented!
